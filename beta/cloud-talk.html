What is Cloud Talk?

Give your business a global voice without boundaries

Cloud TALK is designed especially for businesses to help them intelligently manage callers and for automate self-service options. It can be useful for gathering actionable customer information and meeting customer expectations in a timely manner, thus eliminating wait-times and increasing chances of giving continuous business. A graphical user interface enables administrators to easy drag-and-drop workflow activities in the platform and streamlines dynamic processes.

Cloud Talk Features

Smart business require smarter attributes

Click-to-call

Allow visitors to leave their mobile numbers on website and request call back.

Voicemail

Businesses can receive recorded voice messages left by Callers if missed to attend.

Multi-Language Messages

Companies can send auto-reply messages in different languages.

Real-time Analytics

It allows enterprises to check and monitor and track IVR performance

Customized Reports

Companies can view & listen to recorded calls and can export reports for perusal.

Location-Based Routing

(Only for India) It helps users to divert the incoming call to the nearest branch of the enterprise.

Dedicated Channel

It ensure highest response rate and can attribute to attend every inbound/outbound call.

Cloud Blacklist

An Enterprise has full control of adding or removing of any unwanted contact through it.

Multi Language IVR

Callers can listen pre-recorded welcome sound at first on their respective native language.

Time Based Routing

Helps customers to route calls to designated individuals according to the time of the day.

Advanced Call Forwarding

Stay connected with callers with different and multiple types of transfer options.

Sticky Member

Returning callers can connect with the same extension they had previously interacted with.

In-Call Transfer

It enables to divert an existing active call to another number using a simple transfer button.

Multi-Level IVR

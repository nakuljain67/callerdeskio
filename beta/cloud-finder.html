What is Cloud Finder?

Revolutionize your user experience and engagement

It is particularly for those companies who wish to give flexibility to their clients to track their sale through cloud-based voice automation. It comes with an exceptional solution in which monitoring activities for any of product/service can be done in a very efficient manner with utmost control. It helps to automate various processes that further can increase sales while minimizing human efforts.

Cloud Finder Features

Smart business require smarter attributes

Inbound Tracker

It helps companies to connect with their clients with automatic inbound mechanism.

Outbound Notification

It allows businesses to get actionable responses from the targeted users.

Customized SMS/Voice Notification

Enterprise can get customised template option as per their business requirement.

Data Management

It manages specific database as well as geodatabase upgrade and maintenance.

Less Human Interaction

It reduces the need of human interactions, most of the process is in automation.

Cloud Blacklist

An Enterprise has full control of adding or removing of any unwanted contact through it.

Real-time Analytics

It allows enterprises to check and monitor and track IVR performance.

Customized Reports

Companies can view & listen to recorded calls and can export reports for perusal

Dedicated Channel

It ensure highest response rate and can attribute to attend every inbound/outbound call.

Easily Scalable

It foster quick and easy scalability to expand and grow business as per the need.

About Cloudytel

Making the cloud a better place

Cloudytel is India’s first open cloud voice communication platform. As a Cloud Telephony company, our aim is to redefine business communications through excellent Cloud-based telephony solutions. Our wide ranges of products, ensuring our clients have access to the latest cloud telephony technologies and most reliable platforms. Whether you are public sector, private sector or looking to start and grow your own business, our integrated products are packaged to meet different business needs along with value added services for our valuable customers and to enhance customer experience, rationalize costs and accelerate revenue growth.

What's New?

We bring productivity to your business

  • Multi-level Security Protocols To Keep Data Safe & Confidential
  • Honest & Transparent Pricing
  • Simple & Pay as you Go Model
  • Flexibility to choose setup as per requirements.
  • Elegant, Clean & Sleek Control Panel
  • Powerful REST APIs Built For Developers

Presently, cloud telephony solution providers in India consider only IVR solutions and are not at par with Global players, which offer a range of communication solutions. Cloudytel, with industry and domain expertise, has developed globally useable cloud API to close this gap. Developers can use this API to build complex communication systems and can embed phones into web, desktop & mobile software, thus offering complete solutions.

Our Vision

Provide a simple, reliable, high performance cloud business communication solutions through integrated cloud telephony platform.

Our Mission

To help organizations embrace cloud technologies so they can engage better with their customers in the digital world.

Our History

Founded in the year 2016, Cloudytel is the brainchild of two veterans of the IT & Business Development field. In their previous ventures, Linktel Media Private Limited & Redtons Technologies Private Limited, they created a simple automated IVR solution for their internal usage and eventually building on it further, they conceptualized a standalone company in the form of Cloudytel Private Limited. Bringing along their exceptional expertise and unparalleled assets, they envisioned a complete cloud telephony solution: Cloudytel. The founders, with their rich experience and know-how, add supreme value to the business ecosystem.

Hard Facts-Clear Solutions

We empower brands to use telephony communication to transform their business at all stages of the lifecycle, from acquisition to retention. Our secure and scalable platform allows marketers and customer service teams to execute highly personalized, engaging unified communication and customer care programs across multiple channels. Our offerings include cloud community building to grow a brand’s cloud contact database, voice alerts for optimal customer care, robust system for communication over the cloud to increase engagement and advanced analytics to optimize spend and retarget.
